About Me...

My name is Pierce Issah. I am a front-end developer looking to create visually appealing websites for clients. You can also call me a graphic designer since I have skills in designing posters, logos, and websites. I practice and hone these skills because I am passionate about the design process. The colors, the font size, the spacing, the line work, you name it. I’m very perspective of elements that no one else cares about. This can be traced back to my artistic side, as viewing the work of others has inspired me to create.
